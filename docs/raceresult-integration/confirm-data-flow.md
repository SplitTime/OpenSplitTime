# Confirm Data is Flowing <span class="label">Beta</span>

## Confirm RaceResult is receiving data

Verify that your decoders are actively sending data to the RaceResult platform. In the left panel, go to **Timing** → **Chip Timing** → **Chip Timing** and confirm that readings are appearing as chips are scanned.

## Confirm OpenSplitTime is receiving data

In OpenSplitTime, make sure Live Entry is enabled for your Event Group. To enable **Live Entry**, go to **Admin** → **Construction**. Click the **Status** icon in the new page and enable **Live Entry** feature. 

![OST instruction to Admin/Construction tab](/assets/images/docs/race_result/race_result_doc_5.png)

![OST status icon](/assets/images/docs/race_result/race_result_doc_6.png)

![OST ensure live entry on](/assets/images/docs/race_result/race_result_doc_7.png)

Then go back to your event, head to **Live Times** → **List** and watch for raw times to appear as they are received from RaceResult.
